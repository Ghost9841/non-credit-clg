Around the Reservoir (Photo Feature)

Shankar Giri
2 hours ago
0

The serene beauty of the reservoir nestled between majestic mountains provides a breathtaking view. This photo feature captures the tranquility and natural splendor of one of Nepal's hidden gems.

As the morning mist lifts from the water's surface, the surrounding hills reveal their lush greenery. Local fishermen start their day early, casting nets in hopes of a good catch. The reservoir not only serves as a source of livelihood but also as a recreational area for visitors.

The area surrounding the reservoir has become a biodiversity hotspot, with numerous species of birds making it their home. Environmental conservationists are working with local communities to preserve this pristine ecosystem.
